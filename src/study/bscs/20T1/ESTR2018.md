### What is probability?

Probability is the likelihood of an event occurring. It is a measure of the likelihood that a given event will occur. Probability is a branch of mathematics that deals with the study of random events. It is used to predict the likelihood of future events and to analyze the past. Probability is used in a wide range of fields, including science, engineering, business, and social sciences.

### Important Theorems and Concepts (重要定理与概念)

1. **全概率公式**: P(A) = Σ P(A|B_i) * P(B_i)
2. **贝叶斯定理**: P(B_i|A) = P(A|B_i) * P(B_i) / P(A)
3. **全期望定理**: E[X] = E[E[X|Y]]
4. **协方差**: Cov(X,Y) = E[XY] - E[X]E[Y]
5. **切比雪夫不等式**: P(|X-μ| ≥ kσ) ≤ 1/k²
6. **中心极限定理**: 当n足够大时，样本均值的分布趋近正态分布

### Difficulties and Key Points (难点解析)

1. **条件概率理解**: 理解P(A|B)与P(A)的区别
2. **贝叶斯定理应用**: 解决逆概率问题
3. **随机变量变换**: 掌握Jacobian变换方法
4. **期望的条件计算**: 使用全期望定理E[E[X|Y]]
5. **中心极限定理应用**: 理解何时可以用正态近似
6. **独立性判断**: 掌握独立与条件独立的区别

### Typical Problem Types (典型题型)

1. **概率计算题**: 计算复合事件的概率
